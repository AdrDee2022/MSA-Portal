Happy Patients

How do I book an appointment online?

To book an appointment online, log in to your account, choose your preferred doctor, select an available time slot, and confirm your appointment. If you don't have an account, you can easily create one during the booking process.

Can I book a virtual (telemedicine) appointment online?

Yes, many healthcare providers on our platform offer virtual appointments. Simply select the telemedicine option when booking, and you'll receive instructions on how to join the virtual consultation.

What information do I need to provide during registration?

During registration, you'll be asked to provide basic information such as your name, contact details, and, in some cases, relevant medical history. This information helps us match you with the most suitable healthcare provider.

How can I find a doctor specializing in a particular field?

Use the search and filter options on our platform to find doctors based on their specialties. You can also read their profiles to learn more about their qualifications and expertise.

Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your appointment through your account. Please check our cancellation policy for any applicable fees or guidelines.

How will I receive confirmation and reminders for my appointment?

You'll receive a confirmation email or SMS after booking your appointment. Additionally, we'll send you reminders leading up to your scheduled appointment to help you prepare.

What do I do if I encounter technical issues during a virtual appointment?

If you experience technical difficulties, please contact our support team for assistance. We'll provide guidance to ensure a smooth virtual consultation experience.

Can I leave a review for the doctor after my appointment?

Yes, we encourage patients to leave reviews and ratings for their healthcare providers. Your feedback helps other users make informed decisions.

How do I access my prescription after the appointment?

If a prescription is issued, you can view and manage it through your account. Some prescriptions may be sent directly to your preferred pharmacy.
